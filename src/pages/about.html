The Nightmare Philosophy

Every webpage gets full Node.js access: require(), fs, child_process, process. There is no sandbox. There is no CSP. Any page can read your filesystem, execute commands, and do anything Node.js can do. The red danger banner warns you, but it cannot be dismissed.

This is an experiment: what happens when a browser has zero restrictions?
